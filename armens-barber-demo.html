Who Is Armen

A Barber Who Takes Pride in His Work.

Armen's Barber Shop is a one-man operation by design. Armen is the barber, the owner, and the reason people keep coming back year after year.

"A traditional men's barber shop — walk-in business. The owner is the barber and he's personable and does great work."

This is the kind of barbershop Northeast Philadelphia is known for — no gimmicks, no chain nonsense. Just a barber who knows his craft and his community.
