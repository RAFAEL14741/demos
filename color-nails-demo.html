About Color Nails Spa

Northeast Philly's Nail Destination

Serving the Bustleton and Rhawnhurst community, Color Nails Spa has earned a perfect 5-star reputation by delivering precision, care, and creativity with every appointment.

Our skilled technicians specialize in the latest techniques — from Russian manicures to intricate nail art — in a clean, welcoming environment. We treat every client like a VIP.

  • Certified nail technicians with ongoing training
  • Hospital-grade sanitation after every client
  • Over 200 gel and dip powder color choices
  • Walk-ins welcome — no appointment needed
  • Open 7 days a week including Sundays
