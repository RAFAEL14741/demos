Our Story

A Family Recipe,
A Neighborhood Tradition

Don Giovanni's has been serving Northeast Philadelphia since 1986. What started as a small pizzeria on Castor Avenue has grown into a beloved institution — but we've never lost sight of what matters: real food, real flavors, and real family.

Every pie is hand-tossed. Every sauce is made from scratch. Every visit is like coming home. That's the Don Giovanni's promise — the same one we've kept for nearly four decades.
