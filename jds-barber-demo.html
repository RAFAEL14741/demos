The Story

A chair on Aldine Street that's been there longer than most barbershops in the city.

JD's has been cutting hair in Northeast Philadelphia since 2004. No franchises. No gimmicks. Just a skilled barber who shows up every day and does the work — fades, lineups, tapers, and beard work that keep clients coming back year after year. If you grew up in this neighborhood, you know JD's.

"The kind of place where the barber knows your name before you sit down."

— 22 years of loyal neighborhood clients
