Our Story

YOUR NEIGHBORHOOD
DINER ON
ROOSEVELT BLVD

Ritz Diner has been serving Northeast Philadelphia since 2018 — a family-owned spot on Roosevelt Blvd where the food is fresh, the portions are generous, and the staff remembers your order.

Whether it's a solo breakfast before work or a Sunday family brunch, you'll always get a warm welcome and a hot plate. No rush, no attitude — just good food.
